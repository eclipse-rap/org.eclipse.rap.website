Add support for upcoming Firefox 64+

For many years Firefox has been dispatching keypress events for non-printable keys, but other browsers not. This will change in one of the upcoming Firefox versions (probably 64.0 scheduled for December). As a result, key navigation and selection in many RWT widgets like Tree, Table, List, and Combo will not function as expected. With a big help from Mozilla developers, we are ready with a fix in this milestone.
